About Me

I am a senior Statistical and Data Sciences major at Smith College in Northampton, MA. Though I've explored many disciplines, I most often apply my data science skills to social psychology and issues of social justice.

Many of my projects utilize big data (usually found online and through social media) to explore human behavior and theories of psychology and sociology. I use methods such as web scraping, predictive modeling... As a life-long competitive athlete, I became interested in studying these phenomenon in relation to sports and sports culture. However, if the project involves big data and a question to answer, I'm in.

Outside of my academic interests, I've spent the past three years constantly covered in chlorine as a springboard diver on the varsity swimming & diving team. As a co-captain this season, I'm excited for one more year of learning and evolving as a diver, teammate, friend, and leader.
